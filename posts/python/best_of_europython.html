Best of EuroPython 2017

In July, the EuroPython 2017 conference took place in Rimini, Italy. After watching dozens of talks, I have shortlisted a few highlights on the main topics:

Daniele Procida: "Fighting the Controls"

Comparing debugging in programming and aviation.

Kristian Rother: "Best Practices for Debugging, Testing and Maintenance"

My own contribution, an overview of essential Python tools. I am very happy to finally have a quality recording of myself speaking.

Radoslav Georgiev: "Practical Debugging"

If you can't get enought, this talk is followed by another talk on debugging.
