The following references provide you with basic common knowledge on grant writing. While they don't replace a book, a course, let alone speaking to an experienced scientist about your application, they at least give you a starting point:

  • Ten Simple Rules for Getting Grants

  • The Art of Grantsmanship by Jacob Kraicer

  • A Fellowship Primer (HFSP) by Guntram Bauer

  • Pockets of Plenty - Non-government Funds for Young Scientists by Leslie Knowlton

  • Ten Simple Rules for Aspiring Scientists in a Low-Income Country

Have fun reading!
