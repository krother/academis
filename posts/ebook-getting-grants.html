This ebook contains six brief articles that put you on the tracks towards writing a successful grant application. It covers what is the basis for persuasive writing, and where and when to start writing an application. Three separate parts help you to convince grant secretaries, reviewers, and commissions. Finally, you find a list of Do's and Don'ts.

I wrote the ebook for PhD and MSc students preparing their first grant application. If you are an experienced scientist, I would appreciate your comments so that I can improve future material.
