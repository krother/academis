How can you lead students towards becoming passionate, competent, and highly motivated young scientists? Uri Alon takes a stand saying that building motivation in yourself and others is a craft worth putting into your scientific repertoire. On the Nurturing Scientists website he has collected practical guides for students, postdocs, and PIs on developing your research vision, motivating your team, and giving good presentations.

In his lectures, Uri even plays the guitar, backing up his own words by example: \"Motivated group members experience a full sense of choice: of doing what one wants.\" [Mol Cell. 2010]
