The Academis Website is alive

It took less than a month to create a functional website for Academis from virtually nothing. I think the team did a terrific job, since the Python code and HTML/CSS were created from scratch.

What did I learn?

  • Feedback from your team can help you a lot to conceptualize what you are trying to build.
  • Don't tell your designer which colors to use unless yours are really good. It makes their work difficult.
  • It's a lot of fun to adjust jQuery elements on ThemeRoller.
  • Not everything about a website needs to be decided right away. You might feel comfortable leaving some 'white space' your concept.

Sure, if you want to create a similar site but without the shopping basket and fancy navigation, WordPress is a quicker and cheaper option. Many webspace providers such as HostEurope offer a button that installs WP, and you can apply standard templates with a few clicks, not knowing HTML or anything.

On the other hand, if you want to build a sophisticated data structure or business process, you may find a more customizable architecture like Django more useful.
