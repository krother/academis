Literature on Scientific Presentations

In this article I would like to share the literature I found on giving scientific presentations. The literature contains articles written by scientists and web resources by professional speakers. I deliberately ignored articles that are not available online, so that you can start reading immediately.

Ten Simple Rules for Making Good Oral Presentations
A clear two-page summary of the essentials. Following these ten rules helps to avoid the biggest mistakes. Usable as a checklist.
Ten simple rules for making good oral presentations. Bourne, Phil E. PLoS Comput Biol. 2007 Apr 27;3(4):e77.
Giving Oral Presentations
Online tutorial on structure, slides, and delivery of presentations. Includes questions at the end.
bit.ly/OralPres
How to give a good talk
Explains how to position yourself on the stage in relation to your slides, how to answer questions and many more.
How to give a good talk. Alon U. Mol Cell. 2009 Oct 23;36(2):165-7. doi: 10.1016/j.molcel.2009.10.007.
Making a scientific presentation
More detailed text guide with a detailed section on visual aids.
Chapter 13. Making a scientific presentation.
http://whqlibdoc.who.int/emro/2004/9290213639_chap13.pdf (Document has moved! I am looking for the correct URL.)
How not to give a seminar
Describes many safe ways to lose your audience during a talk. Helps against overconfidence in two pages.
How (not) to give a seminar. Gottfried Schatz. FEBS Letters, Volume 534, Issue 1 , Pages 5-6, 16 January 2003.
Six Minutes
Professional speaking blog by Andrew Dlugan much of which applies to technical/scientific presentations.
sixminutes.dlugan.com
52 Speaking Tips
Series of 52 short audio lessons by Public Speaking Champion Craig Valentine. Sent weekly by email.
www.52speakingtips.com
Powerful Presentations
ebook that gives a step-by-step recipe for preparing talks. Explains techniques used by professional speakers on 14 pages.
Rother, Kristian (2013): Academis Powerful Presentations. figshare.
http://dx.doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.866828
