In any team, seminar group, individual members will sometimes take positions difficult to deal with. Have you encountered the shy, the preoccupied, the logical, the uninterested, or the aggressive type?

In the play \"12 angry men\" you can see these and more characters at the negotiation table. The movie shows how a jury reaches a decision on a murder case and is an example how each kind of personality can be approached and brought to a consensus. Oh, and it's on Youtube:
